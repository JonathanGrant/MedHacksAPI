Epidemics

Creating Global Health Awareness

What: Widespread Occurence of Infectious Diseases
Where:Local Communities Worldwide
Who: Any and All Individuals

Educate yourself

For Example: The Influenza Virus
also applicable for other diseases

Recognize the Disease

Symptoms: Fever, chills, cough, sore throat, runny nose, muscle aches, headaches, fatigue, vomiting, diarrhea


Take Action to Prevent Spread

Step 1: to inform the clinics near you.

Step 2: Practice tactics to avoid disease spread. See column on the right.

Step 3: If condition does not improve in a timely manner, see a physician near you. Text the RES hotline for infomration.

Take Preventative Action

For Example: The Influenza Virus
also applicable for other diseases

Step 1: If already sick,
Otherwise, to get vaccinated.

Step 2: Wash your hands often

Step 3: Stay at home if signs of infection present.

Step 4: Sensibly, use antibiotics to speed up recovery.

Step 5: Be smart about food preparation

Step 6: Don't share personal items.

Step 7: Travel with discretion.

Step 8: Disinfect 'hot' areas often. (ie kitchen, bathroom, etc)

Step 9: Notify your local healthcare provider if conditions change.

Step 10: Spread preventative disease control knowledge.
