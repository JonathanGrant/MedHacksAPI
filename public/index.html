WHY CHOOSE US?

Interindustry Integration Initiative

By linking clinics and sharing information, we hope to increase the effectiveness of infectious disease control and aid in the allocation of resources to those in desperate need.

Powerful Community Impact

Patients meet providers — because our communities come first. By allowing information input from locals, healthcare workers can easily see where need exists and respond appropriately. Through awareness, we hope to increase response and mobilize care.

Innovative Data Optimization

By combining integrated information from both the local community and healthcare providers on a national scale, we provide an effective way to measure and respond to a community's threat level index for epidemic response.
